ROBONIX

Different events categorized under robonix are:

LINE TRACER

LINE TRACER is an event where you need to make a Line Follower Robot, as the name suggests, it is an automated guided vehicle, which follows a visual line embedded on the floor or ceiling. Usually, the visual line is the path in which the line follower robot goes and it will be a black line on a white surface but the other way (white line on a black surface) is also possible.

MAZE SOLVER

This event is aimed to have bots that can solve the maze grid. The maze solving robot — also known as a micro mouse — is designed to find a path without any assistance or help. As a type of autonomous robot, it has to decode the path on its own to solve the maze successfully. So it’s logic is quite different from the line following robot which follows a predetermined route.

TERA-ROVER

The challenge is to build your robot either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions to achieve the maximum speed to beat other robots on the given track and reach the finishing line in minimum time. The robot must run on the given racetrack. The a robot must start behind the starting mark and is considered to have crossed the finishing line if any part of the robot crosses it when the robot has completed a full lap of the course.

ROBO-SOCCER

This event is to see how people can make robots to play football. The robot can play soccer by dribbling and kicking a ball, though not as good as Messi or Ronaldo. It can be controlled using a smartphone with a wireless Bluetooth connection which enables you to make many of them and have a robot soccer match!

ROBO-CARROM

If you thought your robotics were only about climbing twisted arenas and scoring goals, think twice!!! This time the robots will lock horns in another heart-in-the-throat game where the basic idea is to pocket the coins in the least time. Yes, this is the task which your bot has to perform by playing carrom and earning maximum coins to win.

Lensified

Different events categorized under lensified are:

SPECTRUM

Spectrum is the main photography event of Lensified. It is a gallery event. Every year hundreds of photographers from Kolkata (like Photography club Kolkata Cloud Chasers) as well as all over India submit their amazing clicks. The photos are divided into 3 categories: portrait, landscape, and wildlife. The top 2 photos from each category are declared as winners by renowned judges. In 2020 Spectrum broke all its previous records by receiving more than 214 photos.

BIOSCOPE

Bioscope is the biggest event of Lensified. It's a short film competition. The event is decided into two parts prelims and finals. On prelims, the selected short films are screened. On the final day, the top 10 short films are screened chose by the judges.

INSTACLICK

Insta click is the online photography event for Instagram. It's based on the engagement of the competitors. Competitors have to tag their photos on Instagram to @lensified.nsec and use the hashtag #p2f to participate. Photos with the highest likes and comments are declared as winners and all the photos are featured on the official Instagram account of Lensified.
