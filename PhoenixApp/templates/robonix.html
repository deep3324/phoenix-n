Robonix

There are an endless number of things to discover about robotics.

A lot of it is just too fantastic for people to believe. ~Daniel H.Wilson

For ages robots have fascinated people, and as engineers we particularly have a knack for it. The aim of this club is actually to introduce newly made engineers to the world of robotics .With basic introduction to the world of arduino, micro controllers and motors students are guided through a hands-on experience in building manual and autonomous bots. Instructed by trained and tested seniors who themselves were once the members of the club, the newcomers discover skills which they carry on with themselves through the long run.

The bots built by the students of this club are given a platform to compete in friendly competitions in the various tech fest organized by phoenix.

The basics learnt in the club come handy when students represent the colleges in inter college tech-fests in different colleges.

Several competitions take place under this club. Out of them the most famous are an all-terrain manual bot running competition and a line tracer event, both of these bots are the very first ones that the students are taught to make. Other events like robo-carrom and robo-soccer are also included under the club.
