Topic: GIT - Learning Version Control

Time: 07:00 PM to 08:30 PM

With the advent of the new academic semester, Phoenix - The official Tech Club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College brings to you a three day webinar series on GIT- Learning Version Control.

The Cybernix wing of Phoenix will be conducting these sessions via Google Meet. The link to the sessions and their timings shall be notified to the registered student.

On these three days you will be guided through all the nooks and corners of Version Control. You will also be introduced to Git and GitHub. Git is a free and open source distributed version control system designed to handle everything from small to large projects with speed and efficiency.

There will demo projects and lots of other interesting things. On top of it, we shall include certain intermediate to high level projects, on completion of which there will be rewards waiting for all of you.

Special Mention: Everything you have to know about Hacktoberfest 2021 will be mentioned in the webinar.

Hacktoberfest is a month-long event, run by DigitalOcean, Github, Intel and many other organisations, held in the month of October, to celebrate open source software. If you participate in Hacktoberfest, you may get a chance to win some interesting goodies !

There are no prerequisites for these sessions. So do join in!
