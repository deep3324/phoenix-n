Getting Started with Technical Content Writing

Topic: Getting Started with Technical Content Writing

Content writing is a continuous process of learning, carefully gathering, sifting, organizing, and assessing, all while trying to craft something that makes sense for a user. Some of you are interested in blog writing or want to start your journey as a content writer but still have doubts about what is content writing, why it's useful, how to learn, are the free resources good enough or should we purchase paid resources? To answer all such questions and to help you figure out how to start your journey in content writing, Cybernix is bringing together a session on Getting Started with Technical Content Writing. Here we will discuss what is content writing, its importance, how to learn, and how to implement it. All the Speakers will share with you their journey and experience and also will provide you with a suitable roadmap. So if you're someone who's still trying to acquire a knowledge on this topic, this session is for you.
